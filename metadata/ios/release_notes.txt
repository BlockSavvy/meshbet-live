Version 1.0.0 - Initial Release

Welcome to MeshBet Live! This first release includes:

• Bluetooth Mesh Networking
  - Connect with nearby devices without internet
  - Automatic peer discovery and connection
  - Real-time status updates

• Cryptocurrency Wallet
  - Generate secure HD wallet with 12-word recovery phrase
  - Sign bets cryptographically
  - Export and backup your wallet

• Live Sports Betting
  - Real-time odds for UFC, NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, and Soccer
  - Create and accept peer-to-peer bets
  - View betting history and statistics

• P2P Video Streaming
  - Host live streams to your viewing party
  - Watch streams from connected peers
  - Quality selection (Low/Medium/High)

• Push Notifications
  - Get notified when you receive bet proposals
  - Alerts when bets are accepted or settled
  - Stream notifications from your network

• On-Chain Transactions
  - Send and receive cryptocurrency
  - Escrow support for bet stakes
  - Transaction history tracking

Coming soon:
- Multi-chain support
- Enhanced streaming quality
- Group betting pools
- Social features

Thank you for being an early adopter of MeshBet Live!
