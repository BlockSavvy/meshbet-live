# MeshBet Live Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## Overview

MeshBet Live ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

MeshBet Live is designed with privacy as a core principle. All user data is stored locally on your device:

- **Wallet Information**: Your cryptocurrency wallet, including your 12-word recovery phrase, private keys, and transaction history, is stored securely on your device using encrypted storage. We never have access to this information.

- **User Preferences**: Settings such as your username, notification preferences, and app configuration are stored locally.

- **Betting History**: Records of your peer-to-peer bets are stored on your device.

- **Mesh Network Data**: Temporary connection information for Bluetooth mesh networking is processed locally and not transmitted to external servers.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal identification information
- We do not track your location (beyond what is necessary for Bluetooth functionality)
- We do not store your betting activity on external servers
- We do not collect payment or financial information
- We do not use analytics or tracking services
- We do not sell or share any user data with third parties

## Third-Party Services

### The Odds API

We use The Odds API to provide real-time sports odds and event information. When you view sports events:
- Only the request for odds data is sent to their servers
- No personal information is transmitted
- Please review The Odds API's privacy policy for more information about their data practices

## Bluetooth and Mesh Networking

MeshBet Live uses Bluetooth technology to create mesh networks with nearby devices. This enables:
- Peer-to-peer communication for betting
- Local video streaming between devices
- Offline functionality

All mesh network communications are:
- Encrypted end-to-end
- Not stored on external servers
- Only shared with devices you explicitly connect with

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Your wallet recovery phrase is stored using secure enclave technology when available
- All cryptographic operations happen locally on your device
- Peer-to-peer communications are encrypted

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:
- **Delete Your Data**: Use the "Emergency Wipe" feature in Settings to permanently delete all app data
- **Export Your Wallet**: You can export your wallet recovery phrase at any time
- **Disconnect from Mesh**: You can disconnect from the mesh network at any time
- **Disable Notifications**: Manage notification preferences in Settings

## Children's Privacy

MeshBet Live is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
